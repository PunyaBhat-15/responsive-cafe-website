About US

Step into a world of soft hues and sweet serenity, where pastel purples set the mood and every corner invites you to unwind. Our café is a cozy escape for dreamers, creatives, and comfort seekers alike — offering a curated menu of soothing drinks and delicate pastries crafted to delight. Whether you're sipping a lavender latte or savoring a berry-glazed tart, each treat is made with care and a touch of whimsy. It's more than just a café — it's a gentle pause in your day, wrapped in warmth, flavor, and aesthetic charm

  • Cupcakes

    Delicate, fluffy, and full of flavor — these cupcakes are the perfect bite-sized indulgence. Each one is topped with creamy frosting and a sprinkle of joy.

  • Mochi

    A tender, stretchy rice cake with a satisfyingly chewy bite — our mochi is handcrafted from glutinous rice and filled with sweet surprises like red bean, matcha, or creamy ice cream.

  • Macarons

    Delicate French almond meringue cookies with a crisp shell and soft, chewy center — sandwiched with luscious fillings .Each pastel-hued bite is a little moment of luxury.

  • Boba

    A refreshing blend of tea and milk served over chewy tapioca pearls — our boba drinks are sweet, sippable, and full of fun. Enjoy a drink that's as delightful as it is delicious.

  • Cake Roll

    A light, airy sponge cake rolled around a creamy filling, a swirl of sweetness and elegance. Each slice is a soft, satisfying treat with a beautiful spiral finish.
